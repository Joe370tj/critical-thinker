Introduction

Sanlam is a large financial services company founded in 1918 in South Africa, now operating across Africa and beyond. Services include insurance, investments, pensions & retirement, loans, and banking. The goal: help people plan for the future and make smart money decisions with trust, honesty, and community impact.
